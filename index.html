Practical info

Key resources

✈️ Flights

Transavia FHT4PK outbound (Tel Aviv → Paris Orly). Israir #262847351 return. Check Transavia app for any updates to your booking.

🗺️ Loire à Vélo Route

Flat levée paths, excellent signage, Accueil Vélo campsites every 15–25 km.
loirebybike.co.uk

🚆 Emergency Escape

The Train Vélo Loire runs Orléans ↔ Saint-Nazaire all summer with a dedicated bike carriage. Hop on anytime the legs give out.
sncf-connect.com

🏕️ Camping

Look for the Accueil Vélo green label — guarantees bike storage, repair tools, and cyclist welcome.
eurocampings.co.uk

🧒 Kids on the route

Ages 4, 8 & 11 are all doable. Cargo bike for the youngest. 22–28 km/day max. Ride early (7–11am), rest in the heat of the day.

🏥 Emergency

European emergency: 112 · SAMU medical: 15
Get European Health Insurance Cards for all 5 before departure.
