✈️ Getting There

Fly Tel Aviv → Paris (CDG or Orly). Then train to Blois (Paris Austerlitz, 1h20) or Orléans (Paris Austerlitz, 1h05). Fly home from Nantes airport or train back to Paris.

🚲 Bike Rental

Détours de Loire — pick up in Blois or Orléans, drop off in Nantes. One-way rental. Book ASAP for August.
detoursdeloire.com

⏰ Daily Rhythm

Ride 7–11am before the heat. Rest and sightsee noon–3pm. Short afternoon leg optional. The 4-year-old sets the pace — and rides in a child seat.

🗺️ The Route

La Loire à Vélo is one of Europe's best-developed cycling routes. Flat levée paths, excellent signage, Accueil Vélo campsites every 15–25 km.
loirebybike.co.uk

🚆 Emergency Escape

The Train Vélo Loire runs Orléans ↔ Saint-Nazaire all summer with a dedicated bike carriage. If a day is too hot or tough, hop on and skip a section.
sncf-connect.com

🏕️ Camping

Mostly municipal and 3-star sites along the route. Look for the Accueil Vélo green label — guarantees bike storage, tools and cyclist welcome.
eurocampings.co.uk
